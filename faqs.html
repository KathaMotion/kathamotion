Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is KathaMotion.np? KathaMotion.np is a creative studio that brings your stories to life through handcrafted flipbooks and custom-printed gifts. We combine motion, art, and emotion to create timeless keepsakes.

Can I order a custom flipbook? Yes! We specialize in custom flipbooks. Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, proposal, or just a memory you want to cherish — we’ll animate it for you.

How much does a flipbook cost? Prices start at ₨699 for our 2x3 inch flipbooks. Custom and larger sizes range from ₨1200–₨2300. Check our Pricing Chart for full details.

How long does it take to complete an order? Pre-designed flipbooks: 2–3 days. Custom flipbooks: 5–10 days (depending on complexity) We’ll update you throughout the process.

Do you offer delivery in Nepal? Yes, we deliver all over Nepal via reliable courier services. Delivery time is usually 2–5 business days.

Can I see a preview before final printing? Absolutely! For custom flipbooks, we send a digital preview before printing. You can request minor changes at that stage.

What if I want a bulk order for events or corporate gifting? We love bulk projects! Contact us directly for discounts and personalized options for weddings, birthdays, or company events.
